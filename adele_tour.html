Adele Live 2016: Adele embarked on a worldwide tour in support of her third studio album, "25," starting in February 2016 and concluding in June 2017. The tour included multiple dates and venues across Europe, North America, and Oceania. Adele Live 2017: Following a hiatus due to vocal cord issues, Adele resumed her tour in March 2017 with additional dates in Australia and New Zealand. The Finale at Wembley Stadium: Adele performed four sold-out shows at Wembley Stadium in London in June and July 2017, marking the conclusion of her Adele Live 2016/2017 tour. One Night Only Concert Special: Adele performed a televised concert special titled "Adele One Night Only" which aired in November 2021. The special featured Adele performing songs from her new album "30" as well as some of her previous hits. These are just some of the major events and concerts Adele has been involved in up until 2022. For a comprehensive list of all her performances, including dates and locations, you may want to consult Adele's official website or reliable concert databases.
